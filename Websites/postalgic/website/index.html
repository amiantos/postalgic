What is Postalgic?

Postalgic reimagines blogging in the spirit of the late 90s/early 2000s web publishing, before social media took over our digital lives.

Online 'social networks' have proven themselves incapable of nurturing meaningful human relationships. We do not need them.

With Postalgic, you can create and manage your own micro-blog through a simple iOS app, generate a clean static site with customizable templates, and publish it wherever you want. No algorithms, no ads, no surveillance—just pure self-expression.

Key Features

  • iOS App: Create and manage your blog posts from your iPhone
  • Decentralized: Host your blog wherever you want (AWS S3, SFTP, and more)
  • Static Sites: Generate fast, secure static sites using Mustache templating
  • Own Your Content: No corporate platform owns your thoughts
  • RSS Ready: Follow others and be followed through good old RSS

A Different Way to Connect

Instead of likes, comments, and algorithms, Postalgic encourages alternative forms of meaningful interaction:

  • Use RSS to follow blogs you care about
  • Write response posts instead of shallow comments
  • Share content you appreciate with your personal networks
  • Communicate directly via email for deeper conversations
